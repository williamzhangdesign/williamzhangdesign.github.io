About me

Throughout my life, I've always been motivated and driven by creativity. This semester, I founded a professional multi-disciplinary fraternity at Michigan known as Alpha Theta Delta, bringing students from a variety of design backgrounds together.

Over the years, I found my passion in creating digital experiences to help people, striving to contribute towards a more accessible, convenient, and pleasurable digital world.

In my free time, I love making music with my clarinet, organizing and arranging music for my live cover band to perform, and bringing people together through music. I currently play the clarinet and saxophone in the Michigan Pops Orchestra and Blue Bops Jazz Orchestra. I've also performed in the Michigan Marching Band, Fanfare Band and University Band in the past.
