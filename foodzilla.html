Foodzilla

Timeline: 3 months, Oct - Dec 2019

Role & Context: Designer, Class Team Project

Foodzilla serves as a platform to connect hungry students with leftover food, helping to
bridge the gap between food waste and food insecurity.

Problem Statement

At universities and colleges, many events end up with leftover food that ends up getting thrown away and wasted. At the same time, many students go hungry throughout their day.
